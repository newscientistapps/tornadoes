America's deadly tornadoes

With the confirmed death toll of 51 certain to rise, the tornado that hit Moore, Oklahoma (see marker), on 20 May could rival the 2011 twister that devastated Joplin, Missouri, as the deadliest since detailed records have been kept.

Sadly, the US is no stranger to such devastation, as this map of fatal tornadoes from 1950 to 2012 reveals. Hover over the circles, which are scaled according to the number of fatalities, to lean more about each event, and view the death toll per year on the chart below.
